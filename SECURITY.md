## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of `po-invoice-app` seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible.

### How to Report
- **Preferred Method:** Please use GitHub's **Private Vulnerability Reporting** feature. This allows you to disclose the issue privately and allows us to collaborate on a fix securely.
- **Alternative Method:** If you cannot use the private reporting feature, please contact us via email at [insert-your-email@example.com].

### What to Include in Your Report
To help us triage and fix the issue faster, please provide:
- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce the issue (e.g., specific inputs, configurations, or URLs).
- Affected version(s) of the application.
- Any suggested remediation or mitigations, if known.

### What to Expect
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within [e.g., 3-5 business days].
- We will investigate the issue and communicate our findings and timeline for a fix.
- Please do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have worked together to address it.
